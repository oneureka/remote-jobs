# About

**Remote Jobs** is a curated directory of remote-friendly companies and job opportunities.

We hand-pick listings from companies that embrace distributed work, helping you find roles that fit your lifestyle — whether you're a digital nomad, a work-from-home parent, or just someone who values flexibility.

## How it works

- Each listing includes the company, position, location, and salary range
- Jobs are sourced from company career pages and verified remote-friendly postings
- New listings are added regularly

## Disclaimer

We are not affiliated with any of the listed companies. This is an independent, curated collection meant to simplify your job search.

For inquiries or suggestions, reach out via the project's repository.
