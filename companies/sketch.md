# Sketch

Sketch is a macOS design toolkit made by designers, for designers, for designing, prototyping, collaborating, and handing off designs to developers.

Sketch 是一个 macOS 设计工具包，由设计师为设计师打造，用于设计、原型制作、协作以及将设计交付给开发者。
