# Anomaly

Anomaly (Anomaly Innovations Inc.) is a software company that builds open-source developer tools including OpenCode, the leading open-source AI coding agent, and SST, a framework for building full-stack serverless applications. The company is founded by University of Waterloo alumni and backed by Y Combinator, Reid Hoffman, Max Levchin, and others. Anomaly's team is distributed across the United States, Germany, Denmark, Canada, and the United Kingdom.
