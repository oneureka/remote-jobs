# Discord

Discord is the communications platform that enables you to build meaningful connections around the joy of playing games through voice, video, and text features. It is a group chat platform that lets you customize your own space to talk, play, and hang out, used by over 90 million friends and communities every day.

Discord 是一个通信平台，通过语音、视频和文本功能，让你围绕游戏的乐趣建立有意义的联系。它是一个群聊平台，让你可以自定义自己的空间来聊天、玩游戏和社交，每天有超过 9000 万朋友和社区在使用。
